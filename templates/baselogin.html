Welcome to Technology Students's Gymkhana

Technology Students’ Gymkhana is the hub of the numerous extra-curricular and co-curricular activities in IIT Kharagpur ranging from sports to socio-cultural. The Gymkhana is managed by the students, for the students, under the guidance and active participation of the faculty and staff members. Our goal is to bring overall development in IITians through cultivating and nurturing their extra-curricular talents.
